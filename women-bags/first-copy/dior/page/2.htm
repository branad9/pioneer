Lady Dior Bag - Sophistication & Style Found the Perfect Destination

The Lady Dior bag, originally known as the Chouchou purse, was renamed in honor of Princess Diana in 1996. The then-First Lady of France, Madame Bernadette Chirac, gifted the princess the Chouchou purse from Dior, which quickly became a staple in her wardrobe. Princess Diana was often spotted wearing the bag in public, including at the Met gala in 1996 where she requested a custom-made navy blue version to match her eye color. As a result, Dior decided to rename the bag in her honor. In 2022, Dior announced they will reintroduce the limited edition of the Lady Dior bag, which has also been worn by many celebrities such as Jennifer Lawrence, Kate Middleton, Kim Kardashian and Kangana Ranaut. The bag continues to be a timeless and chic piece, solidifying the connection between Dior and the British monarchy.

The Rich History of the House of Dior

Christian Dior is a French fashion designer who founded the House of Dior in 1947 and is widely considered one of the most influential figures in the history of fashion. His designs are known for their elegance, sophistication, and femininity, making them popular choices for red-carpet events and formal occasions. Dior's iconic “New Look” silhouette, featuring a fitted bodice and full billowing skirts, was especially popular in the 1950s and remains a classic look today. The brand's reputation for quality and craftsmanship, using luxurious materials and impeccable construction, have made Dior's garments coveted items in the entertainment industry. The brand's attention to detail and commitment to excellence have helped Dior maintain its status as a leader in the fashion industry. Many celebrities have been seen wearing Dior, including Jennifer Lawrence, Kim Kardashian, Selena Gomez, Gigi Hadid and Olivia Palermo, who all have incorporated Dior's iconic bags in their own unique and stylish ways.
