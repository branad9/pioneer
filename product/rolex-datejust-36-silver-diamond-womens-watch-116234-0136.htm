Style and class at your fingertips. The Rolex Datejust 36mm 116234-0136 is an authentic Swiss-made Women’s luxury watch that features a polished 904L Oystersteel stainless steel case and a matching brushed with polished 904L Oystersteel stainless steel oyster bracelet with a folding oysterclasp deployment buckle. The stunning silver dial is adorned with 10 diamond hour markers, and a date aperture at the 3 o’clock position encompassed by a solid 18k white gold fluted bezel. This durable watch is driven by COSC Superlative Chronometer Certified Self-winding automatic movement with a 48-hour power reserve.
