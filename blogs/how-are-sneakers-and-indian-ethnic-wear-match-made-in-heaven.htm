How Are Sneakers and Indian Ethnic Wear Match Made in Heaven?

Whenever it comes to pairing Indian outfits with the right luxury shoes, you usually reach out for Mojris, Kolhapuris, Sandals, Wedges, or even stylish flats or Slippers or the same old heels. Undoubtedly these are timeless looks and easy to carry. But of course, a little experiment never hurts. Right? So if you want to spruce up your Indian wear then sneakers can help you achieve a funky yet elegant look. The trends are changing, and bring on your A-game during the festivities or wedding season. The best part is this style is for everyone at every age. If you are wondering how to rock sneakers with Indian wear then read this article till the end!

A Complete Styling Guide on How To Pair Sneakers With Different Indian Attire

There is not one or two but a wide range of Indian wear. However, this article will talk about 5 common and widely worn Indian clothes that you can seamlessly pair with sneakers.

Styling Sneakers with Indian Wear for Women

1. Sneakers and Lehengas

Are you a would-be bride or bridesmaid and want to dance the night away? If yes, then you have to try pairing sneakers with lehengas. Bridal sneakers have readily caught the eye of modern brides who do not mind tweaking traditions. These sneakers are decorated with crystals, embroidery, and other embellishments to make you look stylish without compromising comfort. You can even get them customized.

2. Sneakers with Sarees

Not just lehengas, but sarees too fit beautifully with sneakers. Embroidered or simple sneakers look fantastic with sarees. If you do not wear sarees often and fear falling down managing the pleats, then pick a sneaker for some added comfort. Bollywood celebs are often seen rocking this look.

3. Sneakers with salwar kameez

If you think sneakers and salwar kameez are for old women, then you are highly mistaken. Comfort is ageless, and so is the sneaker-salwar kameez combo. This is a neat look that allows you to stay active all day long without any foot ache. You can wear a classic white sneaker with your trendy salwar or don shoes of complimenting colors as you twirl in your Anarkali salwar. 

Styling sneakers with Indian wear for men

1. Sneakers with Sherwanis

Sneakers are not just for the bride. Grooms and groomsmen can also join in the fun. Be it your Haldi ceremony or D-day, there is a sneaker for every event. You can go with simple white or light color sneakers for the day and jazzy neon ones for the night. The best part is you can reuse them for vacations or daily use.

2. Sneakers with Kurta Pajamas

Do you love to wear kurta pajamas, but can never find the perfect shoes to complete the look? Well, sneakers are always there to your rescue. They go well with chikankari or solid-color kurtas and white pajamas for an elegant look. However, you are always free to jazz it up and experiment until you find the perfect match. 

The Bottom Line

Honestly, sneakers and Indian wear are a match made in heaven. Now that you know how to style sneakers with gorgeous ethnic wear, get your hands on the luxury sneakers from Balenciaga and get ready to rock the party!
