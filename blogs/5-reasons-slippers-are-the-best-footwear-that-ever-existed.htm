5 reasons slippers are the best footwear that ever existed

There are numerous footwears that glam up your outfit when you step out of your home. Be it Loafers or sandals for Party wear or Boots in winterwear. No wonder you love shopping for new styles and colors to match your wardrobe! But what about your feet when you are at home? Essentially, a shoe protects your feet, and this is not limited to the outdoors.

In case you are wondering what should you wear indoors, the answer is slippers. They are probably the best footwear to ever exist. They are casual, convenient, and cozy. If you do not wear slippers this article will change your mind. 

Why You Should Wear Slippers at Home? 

Slippers can become your best friend at home for the following reasons.

1. Slippers Reduce Your Chances of Catching a Cold

Believe it or not, this is true. Not just during the winters, but throughout the year floors are usually cold, especially the ones made of marble or tiles. The body heat escapes from the feet, and you can easily fall sick from cold feet. Slippers keep the feet warm and retain heat.

2. Slippers Minimize the Chances of Infections and Injuries

Another reason why slippers are a must at home is that they protect your feet from microscopic bacteria causing infections. Additionally, slippers also reduce the risk of injuries by improving your grip and balance. If you have kids at home then wearing slippers will save your feet from scattered toys, spilled juices, and broken objects.

3. Slippers Prolong the Life of Your Socks

If you like wearing socks to keep your feet warm during the winter, then do not forget to pair them with slippers. Slippers keep the socks dry and prevent them from getting dirty or torn, hence prolonging the life of the socks. 

4. Slippers Are Super Easy to Wear

The best part about slippers is that they are extremely convenient to wear. There are no back straps so you can simply glide your feet in and you will be good to go. You can also have a pair of slippers for the outdoors when you run some errands.

5. Slippers are Easy to Clean and Maintain

There are not too many times that you wash your shoes. However, closed feet shoes can have bacterial growth causing your feet to smell if they sit in your wardrobe for a long time. Additionally, sweat can also cause a foul odor. If you spill something on your shoes or step in mud, cleaning your favorite pair can be a task. However, with slippers, you do not have to worry about these things. These are open shoes and can be cleaned in a jiffy with some running water and dried in the sun. Unless, of course, you are wearing fluffy fur slippers. These are ideal to be worn at home. 

The Bottom Line

The 5 reasons above not only advocate the benefits of slippers but also tell you why they are the best footwear to ever exist. Now glam up your indoor style with Balenciaga Home Furry Logo Slippers and experience happy feet every day.
