Essential hand jewellery women need at every stage of their life

Every time you dress up, you will notice that no look is complete without the right kind of sunglasses and accessories. Sometimes a statement necklace or earrings may not be sufficient to achieve the glam you want. So what should you do?

The answer is hand jewelry. Hand jewelry does not discriminate, there is something for every woman at all stages of her. Right from brunches, and movie dates to weddings and office meetings, no matter what is the occasion or outfit, you can always find a piece of hand jewelry to complement your looks. Also, GG belt can beat your jewelry anytime pairing it with saris and skirt will just make you feel like royalty. There are 5 must-have handpieces of jewelry that every woman needs, and this article will tell you all about them. 

5 Hand Jewellery Every Women Needs In Her Vanity For Every Occasion

Each of these pieces of hand jewelry has been around for a long time and evolved with changing fashion trends. They are:

  1. Bangles

Bangles are rigid bracelets that have no closure and simply slide onto your wrist. You can find bangles made from different materials like gold, copper, brass, and glass. Each of these bangles holds a very special place in a woman's life. In Indian culture, bangles are believed to be auspicious and hold meaning. You can pair them with any traditional outfit like a saree, lehenga, or salwar suit. 

  1. Bracelets

Unlike bangles, bracelets usually have a clasp or hook closure. These are made of sturdy materials like metal. However, sometimes enamel, tortoiseshell, wood, and plastic are also used. Some are embellished with intricate designs, while some are decorated with charms, gemstones, ivory, and more. You can wear a bracelet with almost anything starting from pencil skirts and shirts to summer dresses and  beach shorts.

  1. Haath Phool 

It is probably one of the most gorgeous pieces of ornament you can own, and it is definitely a wedding essential. Haath Phool is a bracelet with chains that go up to connect with the fingers. It is more like an amalgamation of a bracelet and rings. Be it your wedding or your friend's, a haath phool is a must for the ladies. Although traditional haath phools were made with heavy designs and embellishments, today you can also find minimal designs that can be worn during the festivities. 

  1. Rings

Whenever you think of a ring, your mind may wander off to the engagement rings. Well, that is not true because rings are one of the most versatile pieces of hand jewelry with a vast variety. There are cocktail rings, adjustable rings, stackable rings, cluster rings, and finger claw rings. Basically, it is a never-ending list. Rings can be made from different materials including wood, shell, metal, clay, and plastic. Again you can wear them to your office as well as a party or a wedding, and they will never feel out of place. 

  1. Armlets

Armlets are similar to bracelets but for the upper portion of the arm. These are usually made of metal, although nowadays other materials are also used, for instance, felt, cloth, and feathers for a boho look. Armlets have intricate designs and are worn above the elbow. These look gorgeous with sleeveless jumpsuits, dresses, and blouses. 

The Bottom Line

 

Hand jewelry is a versatile piece of ornament, and the maker is only limited by their imagination when making one. You can experiment with pieces of hand jewelry as you want to add a new dimension every time you step out. A Swarovski bracelet or a Gucci ring is sometimes the only thing you need to bedazzle someone.
