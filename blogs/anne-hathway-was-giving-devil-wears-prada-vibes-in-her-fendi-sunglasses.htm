Anne Hathway was giving Devil Wears Prada vibes in her Fendi sunglasses

If you haven't seen the 2006 movie, the Devil Wears Prada, let me tell you about it. The story is rumored to be about Anne Wintour, the Vogue editor-in-chief. Her role was played by Miranda Priestly. Hathway played the role of assistant of Priestly, who ran Runway magazine. Andrea, the assistant slowly wins over Priestly's character by becoming fashionable. Wearing thigh-high boots and some gorgeous sandals.

Why Does Her Recent Look Remind Everyone Of  "Devil Wears Prada?"

 

It all went down in the recent Michael Kors show showcasing the 2023 spring/summer line. Anne was seen wearing a black turtleneck with a crocodile skirt in brown. The color matched her knee-length jacket. 

The outfit sparked reminders of her character with her messy bangs accompanied by a high ponytail. She was also seen in Fendi sunglasses along with the whole ensemble. People said it was a reference to her in the last scene.

The last scene showed Anne's character walking down the streets of New York City. She wore a brown leather jacket over a black turtleneck with jeans. She also wore brown boots ending just below the knee. 

The hairstyle was also similar which made media and fans go crazy. People online commented things like " Andy Sachs, is that you??" 

The craze intensified when Anne was seen sitting next to the longest-serving editor-in-chief of Vogue. The vision of Anne Hathway and Anne Wintour sitting together caused more enthusiasm. 

People were talking about how she seized the chance when she saw it by sitting beside Anne. Her stylist also said that more than the clothes, the people were connecting with Anne. Anne from the movie and in real life and the connection. 

Erin Walsh, her stylist also noted that she is thought of as a fashion icon since Devil Wears Prada. They said that they were going to give the audience the fashion icon side of Anne Hathway more and more. 

Anne: The Fashion Legend

 

It is to be noted that Anne has always been a fashion legend. She has always been a headline grabber for her fashion choices. She has also stunned the media with her Ralph Lauren gown at MET Gala 2015. She made headlines that time wearing a gold hooded gown.

 Another memorable instance was during the pop-up for Amazon's Museum Of Love in 2019. She wore an Oscar De la rental gown in white while showing off her baby bump. Recently she caused an uproar on Cannes Red Carpet by wearing an Armani Prive gown and a 107.5-carat diamond necklace. She was promoting her film Armageddon Time there. 

Anne and her fashion choices continue stunning the audiences. While she has not made any statements as to whether the look and sitting beside Anne Wintour were intentional, fans kept speculating. It seems like she's been living the Andy Sachs life, after all, dazzling everyone with her glamour and charm! Well, we can't fault her, can we, as long as she dazzles us with her amazing fashion choices which we love!
