How to rock Wedges Like a Fashion Diva?

Some outfits can be only complimented with heels. But stilettos or pumps can leave you with foot aches. And Slippers,Sneakers, and shoes are sometimes way too safe choices when you want to experiment, So what is the way out? The answer is Wedges. 

Wedges are a mixture of comfort and style in one shoe. These shoes are raised at the entire sole and give support to your feet as you dance the night away. They come in a variety of styles and colors perfect for office meetings to parties, and date nights. You can pair them with a saree or trousers and turn heads wherever you go.

6 ways you can style your Wedges and look like a star

If you are wondering how you can style wedges in your daily life, then here is a guide on how you can style different types of Wedge shoes with any outfit.

1. Peep Toe Wedges

These wedges give you a very chic and professional look. You can wear them to work every day with pencil skirts, pants or dresses. Peep toe wedges also go with salwar kameez and sarees.

2. Boot Wedges

If you love vacationing in the mountains and want to wear something that keeps your feet warm, yet you look stylish, then boot wedges will help you achieve that perfect look. The length of boot wedges usually varies and can be knee-high. Hence you can wear the shorter ones with pants and the higher boot wedges with dresses and skirts.

3. Wedges Sneakers

What happens when someone tries to blend fancy with athletic? Well, the resulting product is wedge sneakers. Yes, you read it right! Wedge sneakers are a celebrity favorite as they are comfortable, stylish, and trendy. These are a perfect fit for a sporty look and can be worn with denim shorts and jeans. 

4. Backless Wedges

These wedges are perfect for a day at the beach or a casual brunch with friends or even running errands in style. You can simply slip your feet in and you will be ready to go. They are easy to wear since there are no backstraps or strings. You can wear versatile backless wedges with almost anything.

5. Espadrilles

You can often catch celebrities wearing these wedges with a summer dress for a breezy look. In Espadrilles, the sole is made of ropes and the top part is made of cloth. You can wear it for date night or movies with friends. However, reserve these wedges for the Summers because you cannot risk getting these wet because of their materials.

6. Clear Wedges

Talking about wedges, and not mentioning clear wedges would be a fashion crime. Clear wedges have caught the attention of many from celebrities to fashion influencers for all the right reasons. These shoes have transparent straps and sometimes a transparent heel too. They look super trendy and can instantly uplift any outfit. 

The Bottom line

Wedges come in different styles, colors, and materials that suit all your needs. Your wardrobe is definitely incomplete without Zara wedges or Balenciaga Espadrilles. Be it an office meeting, pool party, date night, or brunch, there is a perfect pair of wedges for everyone.
