10 Best Winter Boots to try in 2023

10 Best Winter Boots to try in 2023

Boots have been in fashion since the later part of the 1960s. Since the 1970s, it has become a staple in wardrobes just like loafers. The reason is simple, fashion and function. You can style boots as well as use them to walk on hills, muddy plains, or snow. Unlike sneakers, this variant of footwear is not practical to wear everywhere yet somehow it shows class and elegance in winter when your outfit can make little to no effect. Let's look at the 10 best winter boots to try now that the winter season is here.

Best Winter Boots for Children

Children are prone to catch coughs and colds easily. Let's look at some great options for kids. 

A.Reima Coconi

These boots are warm, waterproof, and have a snug fit. They are waterproof, have great traction, and are perfect for running around. You can even hike in these boots. 

B. Merrell Snow Quest Lite 3

These are all-rounder boots. If you have a backyard or want to go sledding, these are perfect. It's also waterproof, keeps feet warm, and is easy to wear.

Best Winter Boots for Men

A.Blundstone Men’s 510 Chelsea Boots

They are water resistant and good for moderate winter conditions like occasional light snow or puddles. They are uninsulated and have elastic gussets and lugged rubber soles.

B. Wolverine 1000 Mile Plain

Toe Rugged Boot- These are vintage looking, age well and have rubber heels. The lugged outsole helps in keeping footing steady on ice on sidewalks and slippery concretes. The leather also creates an attractive patina as time passes.

C.Vasque Sundowner GTX Boots

They can be styled for both formal and informal wear. They have a waterproof membrane, i.e the covering of GTX. They are simple and have a classic design. They also have a lugged rubber sole.

Best Winter Boots for Women 

A.Sorel Joan Of Arctic Next Boot

 These are made with a combination of suede and waterproof full-grain leather. They are shearling lined and have a semi-sealed construction keeping your feet dry. There is a scallop sole with a mudguard to prevent them from being covered in slush. It has a leather heel pull tab for easy wearing and taking them off. They are also lightweight. 

B.Kamik Women's Momentum 3 Snow Boot

These boots are equipped to handle all kinds of winter. It has a faux leather snow collar and thermal insulation. It can keep your feet warm even in -40°F temperatures. They are also waterproof, having a semi-sealed nylon upper.

C. L.L. Bean Rangeley Waterproof Pac Boots

 The boot blocks out ice and snow with the semi-sealed construction wrap. It also has insulation to keep feet warm and dry.

D. The North Face Shellista IV Mid WP Boots

The boot isn't heavy and you can wear it for long hours. It is 85 percent more insulated with Heatseeker made for recycled eco materials. It also has a waterproof upper body made of leather ballistic nylon. 

E.Hunter Insulated Roll Top Sherpa Boots

If your place often has slushy roads in winter, this boot is great to have. The upper body is sherpa lined which can be adjusted upward or downward. The sole is thermally insulated to keep your feet warm even in extreme cold.

Final Words

These are some of the great boots that you can check out if you are on the hunt for a boot in winter. These classy, simple 21st-century designs will fit right in with your attire.
